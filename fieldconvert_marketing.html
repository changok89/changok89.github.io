Frequently asked questions

Does FieldConvert require an internet connection?

Core conversion use is designed to remain useful even when connectivity is limited or inconsistent.

Which measurement systems are supported?

FieldConvert currently centers on Metric, US, and Imperial handling, including user-selectable system preferences that influence defaults and suggestions.

Is this a general-purpose lifestyle product?

No. The product tone is intentionally utility-focused. It is meant to be practical, clear, and low-distraction.

Can I save repeat conversions?

Yes. The current product direction includes favorites, history, and presets to make recurring conversion tasks faster over time.
