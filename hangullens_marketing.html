Hangeul Lens

Read Korean text with faster travel context.

Hangeul Lens is built for visitors who need a simple way to understand Korean signs, menus, labels, and notices in everyday situations.

Open the camera, scan a photo, and view translated text overlays with a lightweight history for quick reference later.

Core Value

The product focuses on a narrow but useful travel workflow: detect Korean text quickly, translate it into a selected language, and keep recent results easy to revisit.

Key Features

  • Live camera text detection with translated overlays
  • Photo-based text scan for saved images
  • Lightweight translation history
  • English, Japanese, and Simplified Chinese output options
  • Privacy-first product direction with user-controlled saved history

Who It Is For

Hangeul Lens is designed for travelers, short-term visitors, students, and anyone who needs fast help reading Korean text in public or commercial spaces.

Typical Use Cases

  • Understanding subway signs and public notices
  • Reading restaurant menus and store labels
  • Checking printed instructions, posters, and entry signs
  • Reviewing previously translated phrases from recent history
