Hangul Lens

Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Hangul Lens handles information when you use the app.

Overview

Hangul Lens is designed to help users read and translate Korean text. The product direction is privacy-first and aims to limit retained data to what is necessary for core app features.

Information the App May Process

  • Camera input used to detect and translate visible text
  • Photos selected by the user for text scanning
  • Translation history items saved inside the app when the feature is used
  • Basic device and advertising data that may be used by advertising providers to deliver ads

How Information Is Used

  • To detect Korean text and present translated overlays
  • To store recent translation history for user convenience
  • To support optional speech playback for translated text
  • To display ads and support app operation

Local Storage and History

Translation history may be stored locally on the device so recent results can be viewed again inside the app. Users should avoid saving sensitive personal information in captured or translated content unless they are comfortable storing that information locally.

Advertising

Hangul Lens may display banner, interstitial, or rewarded advertising. Advertising services may process device-related information according to their own policies and technical requirements. Users should review the privacy terms of those providers where applicable.

Permissions

The app may request access to:

  • Camera, to scan visible text in real time
  • Network access, to support app services and advertising delivery

Data Sharing

Hangul Lens does not describe its core experience as an account-based or cloud-sync service. However, third-party service providers used for advertising or related infrastructure may process limited technical data as part of their services.

Children

Hangul Lens is not specifically directed to children. Users should review the app listing and store configuration for age suitability in their region.

Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect product, legal, or operational changes. The latest version should be kept with the current app listing or official support resources.
